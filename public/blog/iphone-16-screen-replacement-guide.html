iPhone 16 Screen Replacement Guide (2025)

Complete step-by-step guide using AO7 tools and Genuine Apple parts

November 1, 2025 • Repair Guides • Nexus Tech Team

The iPhone 16 series introduces several new challenges for screen replacement, including the Ceramic Shield front glass, Dynamic Island complications, and enhanced waterproofing. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know for professional screen replacement using Genuine Apple parts and AO7 premium tools.

Tools and Parts Required

AO7 Professional Toolkit
  • AO7 Precision Screwdriver Set
  • AO7 Heat Platform (recommended)
  • AO7 Suction Cup and Pry Tools
  • AO7 Opening Picks (various sizes)
  • AO7 Magnetizer/Demagnetizer
Genuine Apple Parts
  • Apple iPhone 16 Display Assembly (select model)
  • Apple Display Adhesive Strips
  • Apple Waterproof Seals
  • Apple Screw Kit

Preparation Steps

1. Power Off and Discharge

Before beginning any repair, ensure the device is powered off and has been discharged for at least 30 minutes. This reduces the risk of electrical shock and component damage.

2. Remove SIM Card

Use the SIM eject tool to remove the SIM card tray. This prevents any potential damage during the repair process.

3. Heat Application

Apply heat evenly across the screen using the AO7 Heat Platform set to 80°C (176°F). Heat for 2-3 minutes to soften the adhesive while protecting internal components.

Screen Removal Process

Step 1: Create Initial Opening

Apply the AO7 suction cup near the bottom edge of the screen. Gently lift while using opening picks to create a small gap between the display and frame.

Safety Warning

Work slowly and deliberately. The iPhone 16 uses stronger adhesive that requires more force but increases the risk of damage if not handled properly.

Step 2: Insert Opening Picks

Slide opening picks along the perimeter of the display. Focus on the bottom edge first, then work around the sides. Maintain constant, gentle pressure to separate the adhesive.

Step 3: Disconnect Cables

Once the display is sufficiently separated, carefully lift it to access the display cables. Use the AO7 precision screwdriver to remove the screws securing the display connector bracket.

Step 4: Complete Separation

Slowly disconnect the display cables from the logic board. Take care not to damage the connectors or flex cables.

Display Installation

Step 1: Prepare New Display

Remove the protective liners from the new Genuine Apple display assembly. Ensure all adhesive strips are properly positioned.

Step 2: Connect Cables

Carefully connect the display cables to the logic board. Ensure proper alignment and secure the connector bracket with screws.

Step 3: Position Display

Lower the new display onto the frame, ensuring perfect alignment. The Dynamic Island cutout must be precisely positioned.

Step 4: Secure Display

Press firmly around the perimeter to ensure the adhesive bonds properly. Allow 30 minutes for the adhesive to cure before testing.

Testing and Quality Assurance

Display Functionality Test

  • Power on the device and test all display functions
  • Check touch responsiveness across the entire screen
  • Verify Face ID functionality (if applicable)
  • Test all sensors including proximity and ambient light

Waterproofing Verification

  • Inspect all waterproof seals for proper installation
  • Check for gaps around the display perimeter
  • Verify IP68 rating is maintained

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Pro Tips

  • Never rush the adhesive separation process
  • Always use genuine Apple parts for warranty compliance
  • Double-check cable connections before powering on
  • Clean all adhesive residue thoroughly
  • Test functionality before returning to customer

Troubleshooting

Display Not Responding

If the touchscreen isn't responding, check cable connections and ensure the display connector is properly seated.

Lines or Dead Pixels

Display defects indicate a faulty display assembly. Replace with a new Genuine Apple part immediately.

Face ID Issues

Face ID problems are often caused by improper alignment of the TrueDepth camera system. Recheck positioning and connections.

Why Choose Genuine Apple Parts

Genuine Apple parts ensure perfect fitment, optimal performance, and warranty compliance. Aftermarket parts may cause fitment issues, reduced functionality, and voided warranties.
