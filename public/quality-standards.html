AO7 Testing Protocol

Our proprietary AO7 testing protocol ensures every part meets or exceeds OEM specifications. This comprehensive testing process includes:

1
100% Functional Testing

Every component undergoes complete functional verification to ensure proper operation.

2
Visual Inspection (40x Magnification)

High-resolution microscopic inspection for surface defects, alignment, and quality.

3
Color Accuracy & Touch Calibration

Display panels are calibrated for perfect color reproduction and touch sensitivity.

4
Water & Dust Resistance Verification

IP-rated components undergo pressure testing to ensure water and dust resistance.

5
Thermal Stress Testing

Components are tested under extreme temperatures to ensure reliability.

6
Drop & Impact Resistance

Physical durability testing to simulate real-world usage conditions.

7
Final Quality Assurance

Comprehensive final inspection and packaging with quality assurance seal.
