Return Process

Getting a return started is simple. Follow these easy steps:

1
Contact Our Team

Email returns@nexustechhub.com or call our support team with your order number and reason for return.

2
Receive Return Authorization

We'll provide a Return Authorization (RA) number and shipping instructions within 24 hours.

3
Package Your Return

Securely package the item in its original packaging. Include the RA number and order details.

4
Ship It Back

Use the provided prepaid label or ship to our returns center. We recommend using tracked shipping.

5
Receive Refund/Exchange

Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund or send your exchange within 3-5 business days.

Return Conditions

Eligible for Return

  • Unused parts in original packaging
  • Parts received within the last 30 days
  • Items in resalable condition
  • Defective or damaged parts (anytime)
  • Incorrect items shipped by us

Not Eligible for Return

  • Parts that have been installed or used
  • Items damaged due to misuse or modification
  • Special order or custom items
  • Opened adhesives, tapes, or consumables
  • Items missing original packaging
  • Parts returned after 30 days (unless defective)

Warranty Claims

Our lifetime warranty covers manufacturing defects and performance issues. For warranty claims:

  • Contact us within the warranty period
  • Provide photos of the defect or issue
  • Include your original order number
  • We'll provide free replacement or full refund
